Welcome to
Pita My Shawarma!

We bring the vibrant flavors of Lebanese street food to Lawrenceville. We specialize in authentic, (halal) Shawarma made fresh every single day.

From our house-made falafel to hand-cut fries, everything is prepared to give you a delicious lunch or dinner. With indoor bar seating and a beautiful outdoor patio, our fast-casual restaurant is ready to serve you.
